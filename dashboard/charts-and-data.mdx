## Charts

The dashboard renders four Recharts visualizations in a 2Ă—2 grid, each showing a different dimension of the analysis.

### Sector confidence radar

A radar chart showing the average confidence score per sector (0â€“10 scale). Each axis represents one of the four sectors â€” Cryptocurrency, Stock Market, Forex & Currencies, Commodities & Materials. The filled polygon shows how confident the sector agents were across all assets they researched.

### Risk vs. score scatter plot

A bubble scatter chart where each point represents a risk-adjusted top pick. The X axis shows the **risk score** (1â€“10, where 10 = highest risk) and the Y axis shows the **adjusted score**. Bubble size reflects the raw confidence score. Points in the upper-left quadrant (low risk, high adjusted score) are the most favorable for your profile. Each pick is colored by its sector using the sector color palette from `constants.ts`.

### Portfolio allocation doughnut

A doughnut (ring) pie chart showing the Strategy Agent's recommended percentage split across sectors. Each slice represents one sector: `crypto`, `stocks`, `currencies`, `materials`, and `cash`. The percentages are whole numbers that sum to 100 and vary based on your risk profile:

- **Conservative** â€” higher cash and materials allocation, lower crypto
- **Moderate** â€” balanced across all sectors
- **Aggressive** â€” higher crypto and growth stock allocation, minimal cash

### Social buzz bar chart

A bar chart showing the average social buzz level per sector. Buzz is aggregated from the `social_buzz` field on each asset (`"high"` = 3, `"medium"` = 2, `"low"` = 1) and averaged across assets in the sector. The Y axis uses labels Low / Med / High. Each bar is colored by sector.

## Per-asset data

Each asset inside an expandable sector panel exposes the full set of fields from the `Asset` TypeScript interface. These are organized below by category.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Price data">
    | Field | Description |
    |-------|-------------|
    | `current_price` | Most recent price at time of research, formatted as a string (e.g. `"$67,500.00"`) |
    | `change_24h` | Price change over the past 24 hours as a signed percentage (e.g. `"+2.3%"`) |
    | `change_7d` | Price change over the past 7 days |
    | `change_30d` | Price change over the past 30 days |
    | `ytd_change` | Year-to-date price change |
    | `week_52_high` | Highest price in the trailing 52-week window |
    | `week_52_low` | Lowest price in the trailing 52-week window |
    | `market_cap` | Total market capitalisation, formatted (e.g. `"$1.3T"`) |
    | `volume_24h` | 24-hour trading volume, formatted (e.g. `"$42.1B"`) |
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Sentiment">
    | Field | Values | Description |
    |-------|--------|-------------|
    | `sentiment` | `bullish` \| `bearish` \| `neutral` | Overall market sentiment for this asset |
    | `social_sentiment` | `bullish` \| `bearish` \| `neutral` \| `mixed` | Sentiment derived from social media posts |
    | `social_buzz` | `high` \| `medium` \| `low` | Volume of social media discussion relative to normal levels |
    | `social_highlights` | `string[]` | Specific tweets, Reddit posts, or social media excerpts collected by the agent |
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Confidence and source agreement">
    **Confidence score** (`confidence`) is an integer from 1 to 10 assigned by the sector agent:

    | Range | Meaning |
    |-------|---------|
    | 8â€“10 | Very strong conviction â€” multiple sources agree, strong momentum |
    | 5â€“7 | Moderate conviction â€” mixed signals or limited data |
    | 1â€“4 | Low conviction â€” conflicting data or high uncertainty |

    **Source agreement** (`source_agreement`) describes how closely the prices or signals from different sources matched:

    | Value | Meaning |
    |-------|---------|
    | `high` | Sources are within 1% of each other |
    | `medium` | Sources differ by 1â€“3% |
    | `low` | Sources differ by more than 3% |

    `sources_checked` lists the domains the agent consulted when gathering data for this asset.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="News and recommendation">
    | Field | Description |
    |-------|-------------|
    | `key_news` | Array of recent news headlines relevant to this asset |
    | `recommendation` | Agent's signal: `buy`, `hold`, or `sell` |
    | `reasoning` | 1â€“2 sentence explanation of the recommendation, written in plain language |
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Sector-level data

Each sector panel also shows aggregate fields from the `SectorData` interface:

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| `sector_summary` | 2â€“3 sentence overview of the sector's current conditions |
| `sector_outlook` | `bullish`, `bearish`, or `neutral` outlook for the sector |
| `top_pick` | Ticker symbol of the sector agent's single best opportunity |
| `top_pick_reasoning` | Explanation of why this asset was chosen as the top pick |

<Note>
  If a sector agent failed to return data, the panel shows a **Data unavailable** message and the `data_unavailable` flag is set to `true` on the sector object.
</Note>
