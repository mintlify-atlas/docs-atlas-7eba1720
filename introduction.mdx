## What is Tododeia?

Tododeia is a **Claude Code skill** — a reusable prompt-and-tooling package that extends what Claude can do. When installed, Claude gains the ability to run a full multi-agent investment research workflow:

- **4 sector agents** research crypto, stocks, forex, and commodities in parallel
- **1 strategy agent** synthesizes everything into a unified, risk-adjusted investment plan
- Results are served as an **interactive bilingual dashboard** at `http://localhost:3420`

<Note>
  Tododeia is for **informational and educational purposes only**. It does not constitute financial advice. Always consult a qualified financial advisor before making investment decisions.
</Note>

## Sectors covered

| Sector | Always includes | Dynamically discovers |
|--------|----------------|----------------------|
| **Crypto** | BTC, ETH | Trending altcoins, DeFi tokens, AI tokens |
| **Stocks** | S&P 500, NASDAQ | Top movers, catalyst-driven stocks |
| **Forex** | DXY, USD/MXN | Pairs affected by current events |
| **Commodities** | Gold, Oil WTI | Agricultural, energy, metals based on market |

## Requirements

- [Claude Code](https://docs.anthropic.com/en/docs/claude-code) (CLI)
- Node.js 18+ (for the interactive dashboard)
- Active internet connection (agents perform live web research)

## How to trigger it

After installation, just mention investment topics in any Claude Code conversation:

- `"Run an investment analysis"`
- `"What are the best investment opportunities today?"`
- `"Analyze the markets"`
- `"Give me a market report"`
- `"Run tododeia"`

Claude will ask your risk profile, then the agents go to work. The report opens at `http://localhost:3420`.
